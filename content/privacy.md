Short version: **this site collects nothing.**

BikeToLunar is a static website with **no JavaScript, no cookies, no analytics, and no trackers**.
Visiting these pages doesn't create an account, set a cookie, or store anything about you.

## Hosting logs

The site is served by a hosting provider that may keep standard server logs (IP address, page
requested, timestamp, browser type) for security and reliability. I don't use those logs to identify
or track individuals.

## Links to other services

Links to YouTube, the social platforms, and the donation services (Buy Me a Coffee, Ko-fi, PayPal,
Liberapay) take you to third parties with their **own** privacy policies. Once you leave this site,
you're covered by theirs, not this one.
