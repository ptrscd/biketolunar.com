## Wait, you're biking to the Moon?

The *distance* to the Moon — **384,400 km**, the average Earth–Moon gap. Same distance, considerably
less vacuum.

## How is the distance counted?

Only kilometres I actually pedal. Every ride gets logged, and the total on the [home page](/) is the
honest running tally.

## How far are you now?

Check the bar on the [home page](/) — it's always current.

## When will you finish?

When the bar hits 384,400 km. At a few thousand km a year, this is a *long* project. That's rather the
point.

## What bike are you riding?

The full setup is on the [gear](/gear/) page.

## Where can I follow along?

Everywhere — the links are on the [home page](/), and the [blog](/blog/) has the longer stories.

## Can I support it?

Yes, and thank you — there's a tip jar on the [home page](/).
