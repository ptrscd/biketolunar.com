Hi — I'm **Peter**, and I'm cycling the distance from the Earth to the Moon:
**384,400 kilometres**, one ride at a time. The [home page](/) tracks how far I've got.

## The idea

The Moon is, on average, 384,400 km away. Bikes are good at covering distance. So:
can one person pedal that far? That's the whole project — a long, slightly absurd
ride, logged honestly.

## The rules

- Only kilometres I actually pedal count toward the total.
- The progress bar on the home page is the scoreboard.
- I share the interesting bits — routes, weather, repairs — on the [blog](/blog/).

## Say hello

I post along the way on all the usual platforms — pick your favourite from the links
on the home page. If you'd like to fuel the ride, there's a tip jar there too.
